Nurture and outreach

The sequences

Three sequences carry the campaign. Full copy lives in the campaign files; samples below.

1. Database reactivation (448 warm leads)

Seven day sequence: 3 emails and 3 SMS, built on a real scarcity close ("the $1 kit closes soon") plus the 2026 registration changes. Sample email 1 opening:

The NDIS is changing fast. From 1 July 2026, Supported Independent Living and online platform providers must be registered with the NDIS Commission, and that mandate keeps widening through to 2030. Providers who get their foundations in order early carry far less stress into an audit. That is exactly why we built the $1 Compliance Starter Kit.

2. Cold email (2,688 decision makers)

Five touch sequence over 13 days with A/B subject and opening variations, fully compliant (sender identity, physical address, unsubscribe on every send). Sample subject lines: "quick question, {company}" and "{first_name}, a heads up on the 2026 NDIS changes".

3. Kit-buyer nurture (in GHL)

Existing automation: 3 emails and 1 SMS over 7 days after a $1 purchase, then Ruby calls if there is no booking. Drives the buyer to the free Discovery Call.

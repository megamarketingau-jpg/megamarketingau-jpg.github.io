Executive summary

What this pack delivers

Off the back of the 10/06 Meta ads audit, this pack does two things: it lays out the new four-campaign structure the account should move to, and it delivers 5 fresh ad concepts (15 images) mapped to that structure. Each concept tests a distinct lead-gen angle, grounded in the account's best-performing live assets and a competitor scan. The audit's headline finding was that the account has good measurement plumbing but no campaign architecture — it was running an Engagement campaign, not Leads, with budget too thin to learn. This pack is the creative half of the fix; the structure below is the other half.

Campaign setup — best practice

How to build each campaign (ad sets · ads · audience · landing page)

Proven winner in the account right now: the ad "07-May · Maintenance Ads 02" — the "Stress Free Property Maintenance / your weekends weren't meant for this" ad. It is the only active ad with real volume and a conversion: 1,957 impressions, 2.86% CTR, $68.89 spend, 1 maintenance form submit. Reuse this exact ad via its existing post ID (1082079338320132_122101392608338978) in Scaling so it keeps its likes, comments and shares (social proof lifts CTR 10–30%). (Runner-up on efficiency: NDIS Ads 03 — cheapest form submit at $19.88, but thin volume.)

Structure

Advantage+ Audience — no interest narrowing (the signal is in the creative). Location: Greater Melbourne, Victoria, weighted east/south-east (Box Hill VIC, Doncaster, Glen Waverley, Dandenong, Narre Warren) + ~15km. Age 25–65, all genders. Placements: Advantage+ (automatic).

Research recap

What this is based on

Best assets
The single video ad had the best hook (CTR 4.90%, $0.16/video-view). Maintenance "weekends" angle drove the most volume + a form submit. NDIS produced the cheapest form submit ($19.88). (Meta Ads API, ad insights 29/05–10/06.)
Competitors
Jim's Mowing runs the same "Take Back Your Weekend" angle (but outdoor-only). Must Have Maintenance flexes "2,800+ clients" + a property-value angle. Barings goes premium. (Meta Ad Library scan, 40 ads, AU.)
White space
One crew handling inside + outside weekly; the founder/face angle; rating-over-raw-count proof. These shaped concepts A, B and C.
Intent
Maintenance/garden search demand is tiny (10–70/mo) — these awareness-led offers belong on Meta demand-creation + retargeting, not Google search. (a keyword-demand tool, Melbourne.)
Deploy checklist

For Mahbub

  1. Fix the account first (audit P0s): Engagement→Leads, NSW→VIC geo, fund one ad set ≥$20–30/day.
  2. Prospecting: one broad Advantage+ ad set, load A, B, C, D as 4 ads competing head-to-head, each to its own landing page (see table above).
  3. Scaling (CBO): seed with the proven winner ad (Maintenance Ads 02) via its post ID so it inherits social proof → /maintenance-offer/.
  4. Retargeting: one ad set, include 30-day visitors + 90-day engagers + CRM list, exclude the form-submit (converter) audiences; run ad E.
  5. Set CTA to Get Quote / Learn More (not Book Now). Keep LP-traffic (offer pages); do not use Instant Forms.
  6. 7-day read vs the winner's cost per form-submit: pause any Prospecting ad >30% worse; promote the new winner into Scaling via post ID. Kill any single ad with hook rate <20% after 48h.
  7. Commission video versions of the two best performers next cycle (video is the proven hook).

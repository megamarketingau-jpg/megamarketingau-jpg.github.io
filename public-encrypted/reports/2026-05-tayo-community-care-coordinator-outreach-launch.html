🧭 How it works

Straight to the people who refer participants.

A short, friendly email sequence opens the conversation. Interested coordinators get a one-page flyer and a call from Nuradin — no hard sell, just real availability.

📋

Reach coordinators

Around 2,000 Victorian support coordinators, with inner-north Melbourne first.

✉️

A 3-email sequence

Three light-touch emails over eight days, each tested two ways to find the best version.

📞

Reply, then a call

Anyone interested gets the flyer and a call from Nuradin within one business day.

🔒

Your domain stays safe

We send from a separate domain so your main tayocare.com.au inbox is never put at risk.

  • A coordinator receives the first email.
  • They reply to say they are interested.
  • We send the flyer and the referral form link.
  • Nuradin calls to introduce Tayo and check the fit.
  • A participant is referred through tayocare.com.au/make-referral.

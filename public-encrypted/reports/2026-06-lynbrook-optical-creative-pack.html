How to deploy

Upload checklist (for the paid-ads manager)

  1. Stand up the booking event first. Connect the booking event to Meta server-side (audit P0) so these optimise to booked appointments, not clicks.
  2. Concept A → retargeting. Run against the recall database + 30-day site visitors — the warmest audience and the biggest revenue lever.
  3. Concepts B & C → a small prospecting test (broad targeting, no interest narrowing). One concept per ad set where testing.
  4. Equal split within a test ad set; day-7 read against a cost-per-booking target of roughly $30–40. Pause anything 30%+ above the leader.
  5. This is a starting set, not full launch volume. A full launch wants 15–25 videos + 10–15 statics; next cycle adds video + the priced-offer concepts once the compliance items close.
Compliance gate (health-services advertising). These three concepts deliberately carry no price and no discount, so they can run now. Any priced-offer creative (the $199-complete-glasses or 50%-off-second-pair offers) must wait until the special-offers terms page is live and total-price disclosure is in place, and every priced ad must link to that terms page. Copy here avoids clinical-outcome claims and uses gentle, non-urgent framing throughout.
Method

How these were built

Concepts were grounded in the account's own performance read, the brand's customer avatars and approved voice, and a reused competitor scan — then produced through our AI creative pipeline at full upload resolution. Every concept maps to a documented lead-gen angle and an avatar. No customer data or metrics were invented; the audit it builds on is a read-only diagnostic.

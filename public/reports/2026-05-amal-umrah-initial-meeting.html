03 — Voice & the people we speak to

Five voice attributes. Three real audiences.

Buyers transact through grief, family pressure, or a long-held intention to honour a parent. Our voice never adds pressure to those moments — it earns trust by being calmer, more transparent, and more faith-fluent than the category default.

01

Reverent

Treats the rite as sacred. Never transactional, never urgent-discount-coded. Honour beats buy.

02

Australian

Spelling, AUD pricing, AEST hours. Plain language — no British or American idiom.

03

Faith-fluent

Uses Allah ﷻ, the Prophet ﷺ, JazakAllah Khairan naturally. Cites scholars with chapter and verse.

04

Transparent

Names the partner, the institution, the location, the date. Anti-Wakala by design.

05

Calm

Buyers are mid-grief or mid-diagnosis. Voice never adds pressure — only reassurance.

The anti-Wakala proof spec — non-negotiable

Four of the documented 1-star reviews against the category cautionary brand cite fabricated proof, post-payment silence, and brand-identity swaps. Every Amal Umrah video, without exception, includes:

  • Beneficiary's name visible on screen for the duration of the rite
  • GPS overlay showing the Haram coordinates
  • Single-take Niyyah — no cuts, no edits at the intention moment
  • Partner's face visible on camera at the start of the rite
  • Timestamp on the certificate matching the video metadata
  • Independent witness statement at the certificate's foot
Why this matters: cutting corners on any of these six items destroys the entire trust moat. The competitive position rests on un-fakeable proof, not on price or brand polish. This spec belongs in the website, the terms of service, every ad, and the proof video template itself.

07 — How the process actually works

WhatsApp is the surface. PopDone is the brain.

The Mecca-based partner never logs into a CRM. The customer never logs into anything. Both just see WhatsApp threads. Everything else runs invisibly inside PopDone — the same automation engine that already powers four other Mega Marketing clients today.

One sentence — intake form fires the booking into PopDone; PopDone sends WhatsApp to the partner + customer; partner performs the rite and replies with video; PopDone parses the reply, runs the six-point proof check, generates the certificate, and delivers the package back to the customer. No new tooling required for Phase 1.

The flow, end-to-end

Three lanes, seven steps. The partner lane and the customer lane only ever see WhatsApp. PopDone holds every detail, fires every message, and never asks either side to log in.

Three principles that make it easy

📱

Partner's only tool is WhatsApp + a phone camera

No CRM training. No web portal to learn. No Arabic UI to translate. We ship them a booking-ID format, a six-point shot list, and the free Timestamp Camera app — that's the onboarding. After two bookings they hold the SOP in their head.

💬

Customer touches three messages, total

Booking confirmation. Proof delivery. Day-7 follow-up. They never re-enter the beneficiary name, never chase us for a status update, never log into anything. The intake form is the only piece of friction in the whole journey.

⚙️

PopDone does the heavy lifting silently

The native GHL WhatsApp Business integration captures every inbound video into the opportunity record. Workflows parse, route, escalate, and follow up. We're not building anything custom for Phase 1 — we're orchestrating tools already running for four other clients.

The partner's 8-step SOP

This is the document the partner prints, laminates, and keeps with their phone. Translated into Arabic and walked through together during onboarding.

01
Read assignment

Booking ID, beneficiary name (EN + AR), relation, custom duaa text. Confirm receipt in the thread.

02
Prepare name plate

Write the beneficiary's name in clear, large script on the prepared card stock. This is what appears on screen throughout the rite — no separate photo is needed.

03
Open Timestamp Camera

App pre-configured to burn GPS coordinates + time + booking ID overlay into every frame.

04
Single-take Niyyah

Look at camera. State the intention with the beneficiary's name + booking ID audible. No cuts after this moment.

05
Tawaf with name plate

Hold the name plate visible at the shot-list checkpoints — start of Tawaf, between rounds, at the Black Stone alignment.

06
Sa'i with name plate

Repeat at Safa and Marwa. Continue carrying the plate visibly through the seven rounds.

07
Read the custom duaa

At the Multazam, on camera, audible. Read the customer's duaa text in full. Beneficiary's name in the final sentence.

08
Reply with proof

Back in the WhatsApp thread: Done AU-2026-0142 + the video file. That's the entire handover.

What if the proof fails the six-point check? PopDone messages the partner with a polite re-shoot request — at our cost, never the customer's. The customer is told only "proof under quality review, ETA 24h." This is the anti-Wakala protection in operation: we'd rather absorb a re-shoot than ship a defective video. The terms of service (Blocker #03) put this in writing.
